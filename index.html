Three options of rock paper scissor are given for the initial
selection. You have to choose one selection in the given time
frame. The final result is given considering your choice and
the choice given by the PC. Rock wins against scissors, paper
wins against rock, and scissors wins against paper.
When your selection and PC's selection are same, the result will
be draw.
